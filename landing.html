Three-Agent Framework

Every Transaction, Cross-Examined

Three specialized compliance agents debate each transaction and deliver a confidence-scored, risk-tiered verdict.

Agent 1 — IRS

IRS §274 Agent

Applies IRC §274(d) — the $75 substantiation rule. Flags any expense over $75 for receipt and written business purpose. Calculates disallowance risk at audit.

Confidence Score82%
Agent 2 — GAAP

GAAP ASC 360 Agent

Applies ASC 360 capitalization rules. Reviews expenses over $2,000 to determine whether useful life exceeds one year, triggering asset capitalization.

Confidence Score91%
Agent 3 — UNICAP

UNICAP §263A Agent

Applies IRC §263A uniform capitalization rules to production-related expenses. Detects inventory, manufacturing, and COGS categories requiring cost allocation review.

Confidence Score74%
